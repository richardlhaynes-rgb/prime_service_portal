ID: 1
KB Title: AutoCAD: How to Reset to Default Settings
Status: Approved
Problem: AutoCAD is experiencing issues such as: Toolbars or ribbons are missing; The program is slow, freezes, or crashes; Custom settings have become corrupt.
Solution: Close all AutoCAD applications. Open the Windows Start Menu. Navigate to the folder for your AutoCAD version (e.g., AutoCAD 20xx). Click the application named "Reset Settings to Default". Choose "Back up and Reset Custom Settings".
Notes: Source: https://www.autodesk.com/support/technical/article/caas/sfdcarticles/sfdcarticles/How-to-reset-AutoCAD-to-defaults.html
